Who we are!

We are dedicated to spreading the excitement of amateur radio home brewing.

Too often amateur operators today just buy their radios off the shelf and they never experience what its like to build a radio from scratch and make a QSO with it.

This site contains step-by-step guides for building some of today's most popular home brewed radio projects. They are designed to take the beginner from the basic understanding of radio principles into the more advanced topics required to design and build a radio from scratch that they will be proud to show off to everyone in their local amateur radio community.
